Consultancy and Strategy

  • Employability and Skills programmes
    • Advised Saudi government on operational realities of delivering vocational education to women, rural youth, and people with disabilities, focused on ‘service excellence’.
    • Collaborated with management consultancy firms to establish a skills organisation; worked with renowned training providers and course developers to build both an English and Arabic-language MOOC platform.
    • National skills strategy for tailored learning programmes and diversified service offer; achieved SQA accreditation; Modern Apprenticeships and accredited CPD.
    • Managed the product lifecycle of a holistic online assessment tool which determines the ‘job readiness’ of jobseekers and identifies barriers to achieving sustainable employment.
  • Organisational Development
    • Change management: six-month programme resulting in reduced in stress levels, improved job satisfaction, and increased performance.
    • Designed peer-support mentoring & coaching systems to support well-being initiatives.
    • Design of staff coaching programme, supporting culture change to adopt a strengths-based approach, develop resilience and care amongst staff.
    • Quality assurance and risk assessments of strategy, organisation design, and new projects.
